Dog

The domestic dog is a member of genus Canis that forms part of the wolf-like canids, and is the most widely abundant carnivore.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Lifespan: 10 – 13 years
  • Gestation period: 58 – 68 days
  • Family: Canidae
  • Class: Mammalia

The domestic dog is a member of genus Canis that forms part of the wolf-like canids,and is the most widely abundant carnivore.The dog was the first domesticated species and has been bred over millennia for various behaviors, sensory capabilities, and physical attributes.
